Task 1: Optimise Your Audio for Web use

This activity involved cropping and optimising the audio files

In editing my audio clips, other than cropping the sounds in order to reduce the file size. I also altered the volume as to not exceed the sampling rate which would be needed with my expected audio.

Furthermore, I layered together multiple sound files in order to create my character narratives, involving the mediation of sound, making sure one did not override the other in an ineffective way.

I then exported my files in .MP3 format. This is because this file format, with WAV or OGG being preferred quality standard, is supported on every browser.
